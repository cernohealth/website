Welcome to the Cerno Health Beta Program! We’re excited to help you track your sleep with clinical precision from the comfort of your own bed. Have questions about the program? We’ve got answers.

Q: How does Cerno work?

A: Cerno uses your Apple Watch to help you track your sleep so you can better understand your sleep and/or your sleep disorder. Over a 7 day period you’ll wear your Apple Watch every day and night – this tracks your biometrics to better understand your sleep patterns. During a few hours in the morning and the early evening you’ll remove your watch to charge, otherwise you’ll keep your watch on and your Cerno app tracking throughout the 7 day period. You’ll also fill out a few questionnaires during onboarding to evaluate your sleep and wakefulness experience (~5 min), and answer prompts each day. When the 7 day period is complete, you’ll receive a clinical report on your sleep metrics which you can share with your doctor to discuss next steps.

Q: How long does the Cerno Beta Program take?

A: The Cerno Beta Program lasts for one week. Because we need to get a baseline of your body’s rhythms, you’ll also wear your Apple Watch and track during the day as well.

Q: How much time do I need to spend per day using Cerno?

A: You’ll wear your Apple Watch for one week (both day and night) to track your body’s rhythms. Every day you’ll also answer a few prompts on the Cerno app, which takes approx. 10 seconds.

Q: What will I know after I complete the Cerno Beta Program?

A: When your sleep trial is complete, we’ll generate a clinical report with your sleep data in our Provider Portal, which you can share with your doctor. If you would like to be connected to a doctor to discuss next steps, email us at hello@cerno.health and we’ll help you connect with a care team.

Q: Why is Cerno running this Beta Program?

A: We’re opening up this Beta Program to various groups, for free, before our wider launch, to get your feedback and insights on how to make the Cerno experience the best it can possibly be.

Q: What happens to my sleep data?

A: Cerno keeps your sleep data secured in our HIPAA-compliant technology. We also generate a clinical report with your sleep data in our Provider Portal, which you can share with your doctor. If you would like to be connected to a doctor to discuss next steps let our team know at hello@cerno.health and we’ll help you connect with a care team.

Q: Can I track my sleep with Cerno after the Beta Program is over?

A: Yes! You can always start another 7 day tracking period with Cerno, even after the Beta Program is over. We always recommend you to track for 7 days to get the fullest, most accurate picture of your sleep.

Q: Can I use the workout app on my Apple watch while participating in the Beta Program?

A: Yes, you can use the workout mode on your Apple watch. Please just remember to stop recording on the Cerno Watch app before you start the workout app and resume recording after you are done.

Q: Do I need to install any updates to the Cerno Health App?

A: All updates are delivered automatically to your phone as long as you have the "Automatic Updates" option turned on in the Cerno App settings on Test Flight. You can also manually install an update by pressing the "Update" button in the Test Flight App. An app update will stop the recording session on your watch, so please resume recording if you notice that the app has stopped recording after the update.
